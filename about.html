More About Me

I grew up in rural Maine. No, I did not live next to Stephen King, yes the lobstah is delicious, and if you're ever in the area, I highly recommend visiting Reed State Park or the Blue Hill Peninsula & Deer Isle. I'm also a first generation college grad and navigated the entire college process by myself -- if you or anyone you know needs help with their application, please feel free to send me an email.

Outside of research I do a lot of arts and crafts and reading (you can check out my reading list here). My favorite authors are Murray Bookchin, Stephen Jay Gould, and Margaret Atwood. Jacqueline Harpman is also an honorable mention. I'm also an active naturalist and birder with a soft-spot for corvids, vultures, and shorebirds.
